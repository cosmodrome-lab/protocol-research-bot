# Research Quality Protocol v1.0

## Mandatory Checks Before Publishing

### 1. Fact Separation
- [ ] On-chain facts sourced from block explorer
- [ ] Off-chain claims attributed and dated
- [ ] Hypotheses clearly marked as hypotheses
- [ ] Unknown explicitly acknowledged

### 2. Confidence Scale Applied
- [ ] Every major claim has confidence rating A-E
- [ ] No A/B rating without verifiable source
- [ ] Speculation marked E only

### 3. Freshness Check
- [ ] On-chain data: max 24 hours old
- [ ] Governance data: max 7 days old
- [ ] TVL data: max 48 hours old
- [ ] Flag if data older than threshold

### 4. Completeness Check
- [ ] Key actors identified
- [ ] Treasury reviewed
- [ ] Governance history reviewed
- [ ] Contradictory evidence considered

### 5. Forensic Standards
- [ ] No conclusions without evidence chain
- [ ] Alternative explanations considered
- [ ] Insider positioning checked
- [ ] Connected wallets checked

## Red Flags That Require Level 3
- Wallet activity 48h before major governance vote
- Treasury outflow >10% in 30 days without clear justification
- Voting concentration >50% in single address
- Narrative shift without onchain basis
- Team wallet activity before token unlock

## Publication Checklist
- [ ] Quality protocol completed
- [ ] Peer review (if available)
- [ ] Legal review (if accusations)
- [ ] Confidence scale visible in output
